Building a StoryBrand – Donald Miller

Use the power of storytelling to clarify your brand and attract customers.

  • Position the Customer as the Hero: You’re the guide, not the hero.
  • Clear Messaging: Eliminate noise. Tell them exactly how you help.
  • Call to Action: Make it obvious what they should do next.
  • Real-life Application: A startup landing page that says “Get Your First Client in 7 Days – Start Now.”
